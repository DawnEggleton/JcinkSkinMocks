To make corrections to incorrect claims or to update information that has changed through natural character progression, please use the Edit Claims form.

Update Claims

Claim updates will submit automatically and will update based on your account ID, so please ensure this is accurate before submitting. Please give it a few seconds to a minute to update through the system and then check the accuracy of your claims by refreshing the page and looking at the relevant claims tabs.

Note: Alias, name, and group updates will not produce new fields. If either needs to change, simply use the new value in the initial fields and check the correct box(es). The information for these two fields will not change unless you have selected the related option.

Please Update Check all that apply. Reminder that when submitting a change to alias, character name, and/or group, no extra fields will appear as this information is filled out above. Ensure the above information is accurate to how you want it to appear.
