Carnivale

This is a topic title First Last Month 00, 0000 at 00:00 AM/PM
On the corner of the Outskirts, Central Davenport, and the Coastline regions, Carnivale is an old amusement park turned permanent fair ground when the money dried up. Skeletons of old rides are rusted brown, the paint peeling in large swatches, and the actual available activities are all centralized near the entrance to the park. The options now are relegated to simpler rides, like the carousel, a variety of fair games, and the nightly show of trick-trained animals, skilled persons, and - you guessed it - metahumans (formally declared "freaks" in the program).

Witch's Hour

This is a topic title First Last Month 00, 0000 at 00:00 AM/PM
Previously part of cottage country, these were the cabins further in from the Atlantic coast and centralized as a resort of sorts. The value of these properties dropped to near-nothing and they were left abandoned when they wouldn't sell. All the old wood and decorations, mixed with dusty and destroyed personal effects that were left behind, give the place an eerie atmosphere and it's often used for hazing, or as a favourite exploration place of ghost hunters.

The Wilds

This is a topic title First Last Month 00, 0000 at 00:00 AM/PM
Spanning most of the Outskirts, the Wilds are nothing more than half-built, abandoned suburban communities, farms and their land, and rough unmaintained rural roads, mixed with actual untouched nature - usually in the form of forests and hiking trails. It's a beautiful area, if you're into that sort of thing, despite the lack of water that most people come to the area for.
