Please Update Check all that apply. Group, Remove University Program, and Graduate from Hogwarts will not provide extra fields. When graduating from Hogwarts, at minimum group should also be updated. Add/Update University Program, Update Classes/Grades, Add/Update Student Quidditch, and Add/Update Student Leadership will overwrite all current data. Job options do what they say on the button: add without removing current data, update without removing unchanged data, or remove specific jobs. The update and remove job options will load in current jobs that are available only so you do not need to try and match data.
